Aftercare for Thoroughbreds

Importance

Aftercare is a critical issue for the sport because the well-being of horses is so important to our fans and the general public. It is also important to note that Thoroughbreds have proved to be successful in virtually all disciplines of equestrian competition and as recreational riding horses, therapeutic riding horses, and police horses.
